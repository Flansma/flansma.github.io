abstract: >
  A one-hour invited lecture for approximately 65 hospital staff members
  at Yokawa Hospital (医療法人社団 敬命会 吉川病院) in Miki, Hyogo.
  The talk introduced ChatGPT from the ground up, covering its basic concepts
  and usage, practical applications in healthcare settings — such as
  summarizing medical records, drafting patient information materials,
  and assisting literature searches — effective prompt engineering techniques,
  and privacy and security considerations when handling sensitive data.
  The session included a live demonstration of ChatGPT and concluded with
  a Q&A segment in which additional AI tools such as CLOVA Note were also
  discussed. Delivered in Japanese.
